Ingredients:
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 3 eggs
- 3 apple

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celcius.
2. Peel apples and cut it to square pieces around 2 cm each.
3. In a large bowl mix and whip eggs and sugar.
4. Add flour to bowl and mix until smooth.
5. Grease baking pan, put apples inside and pour batter on them.
6. Bake around 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
